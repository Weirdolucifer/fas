Mission

Statement

Fine Arts Society at IIIT-Allahabad educates students and the public in the visual and performing arts and serves as a cultural center for the campus and the surrounding region through creative activity, research, liberal education and public engagement.

by fostering a community of interdisciplinary learners enriched by meaningful experiences with the arts, by integrating innovative technologies as a pedagogical tool, by effectively implementing the liberal education program, by developing interdisciplinary curricula across the IIIT-Allahabad and across campus, and by cultivating an environment that encompasses the full landscape of learning.
